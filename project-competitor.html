Automated Rate Parity & Competitor Tracking

Building a rate shopper dashboard to maintain market positioning against 5 key competitors.

Download Source

The Challenge: Market Positioning

Role: Competitor Rate Analyst (Market Spy)
Goal: Maintain RGI (Revenue Generation Index) > 1.15

In the dynamic Bali market, rates change hourly. Working as the competitive intelligence lead for 3 properties, I needed to ensure we weren't just following the market, but leading it. Manual rate shopping was too slow to react to "flash sales" by our primary compset.

The Solution: Automated Rate Shopper

I utilized **Python** to scrape rate data from OTA public APIs and track parity violations (where OTA rates undercut our direct site). This data was fed into a SQL database and visualized for the Revenue Director. 

# Competitor Rate Simulation
def track_compset_parity(our_rate, compset_avg):
    # Rule: Always maintain premium position (15% above compset average)
    target_price = compset_avg * 1.15
    
    if our_rate < target_price:
        action = "YIELD_UP" # We are leaving money on the table
    elif our_rate > (target_price * 1.1):
        action = "MONITOR_VOLUME" # We might be priced out
        
    return action

Strategic Finding

We successfully maintained our premium positioning (the Orange line sits consistently above the Teal average). However, in Month 4, we encroached too close to the "Market Leader" price point, which resulted in a slight dip in conversion. We adjusted quickly by deploying a tactical "Stay 3 Pay 2" offer.
